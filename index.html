Key Insights & Risk Assessment

Strengths

  • Profitable business — Net profit of 67.77 L with 10.0% margin over 5 months
  • Revenue growing +40.1% — Consistent upward trend from April to August
  • Strong cash position — 4.51 Cr in cash and bank reserves
  • Solid asset base — 24.81 Cr in net assets supporting long-term growth
  • Recovering trajectory — Only April was a loss month; 4 consecutive profitable months

Risks & Recommendations

  • Thin margin (10%) — Vulnerable to cost increases. Negotiate better purchase rates.
  • Revenue concentration — Only 5 accounts = 80% of credit inflows. Diversify customer base.
  • Purchases = 49% of costs — Explore bulk deals, alternate suppliers for 2-3% savings.
  • Asset-heavy structure — High depreciation and maintenance overhead to manage.
  • Build reserves — Maintain 2-3 months of operating expenses as a safety buffer.
